Beware of phishing SMSes and websites

There is an increase in fake SMSes redirecting to fake Singpass websites phishing for Singpass login details. Always check that the URL domain of the Singpass website is singpass.gov.sg before you proceed. Singpass does not send hyperlinks or QR codes through SMSes or WhatsApp.
